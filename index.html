Back-office design directions

Six visual directions for the Thinslice operations back office, each shown as a working preview: a Fields list and a Field detail view with a parcel map. Tap any direction to open it. Works on desktop and mobile.

These are styled previews. The maps are generated illustrations standing in for live OpenStreetMap tiles, which load in the production app. Field data shown is sample data for the Tabora region.
